Meet Dr Fiona Atieno

Fiona Atieno is a young doctor with a passion for technology.
Fresh from completing her medical degree at Kenyatta University, she is
now exploring the world of web development, equipped with foundational skills
in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. With experience as a medical annotator
and a certification from SheCodes, Fiona is eager to bridge the gap
between healthcare and tech, starting with hands-on projects like
building CRUD APIs and interactive websites. Her drive to learn and adapt
positions her well for a dynamic future in health tech.
